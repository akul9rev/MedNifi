The Problem We Solve

Pharmacies lose money and
put patients at risk — silently.

Without Mednifi
Reactive. Manual. Risky.

Most pharmacies rely on manual shelf checks and spreadsheets. By the time a problem surfaces, it is already a loss or a patient safety incident.

  • Expired medicines discovered on shelves after they have been dispensed
  • Stockouts found only when a customer asks for a medicine
  • No visibility into which batches are nearing expiry
  • Hours lost to manual inventory counts every week
With Mednifi
Proactive. Intelligent. Safe.

Mednifi creates a system that continuously monitors inventory, surfaces risks early, and ensures your team always knows what is on the shelf and for how long.

  • Expiry alerts fire 30 days and 7 days before any batch expires
  • Low stock thresholds trigger before shortages go critical
  • Every sale reduces the correct batch, with no manual calculation
  • Full batch history visible at a glance, without shelf counting
How Mednifi Works

From stock entry
to smart decisions.

01 · Add
Every batch entered with full detail
When stock arrives, staff log the medicine, batch number, quantity, purchase date, and expiry. Each batch lives independently and is never merged or averaged.
02 · Monitor
Continuous expiry and stock tracking
Mednifi watches every batch in real time. As stock moves and dates approach, the system calculates risk and queues the right alert before action is needed.
03 · Alert
Your team is notified early — not late
Alerts fire at 30 days and 7 days before expiry. Low stock triggers before the shelf is empty. No surprises and no last-minute scrambling.
04 · Sell
Sales reduce the right batch automatically
When a sale is recorded, Mednifi deducts from the correct batch and prevents selling more than available. Patient safety is enforced in software.
